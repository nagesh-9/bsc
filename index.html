Welcome to Our College

Sri Deepthi Mahila Degree College is dedicated to providing quality education for women, fostering an environment of academic excellence, personal growth, and leadership. Our institution is committed to shaping the future of young women and empowering them to achieve their full potential.

Explore our website to learn more about our academic programs, faculty, and the vibrant campus life we offer.

Established with the vision of promoting female literacy and empowerment, our college has grown into a beacon of knowledge and opportunity. [Image of university campus]

Our Academic Departments

We offer a diverse range of undergraduate programs designed to meet the academic and career aspirations of our students.

  • Department of Science (B.Sc.) - Offers courses in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology.
  • Department of Commerce (B.Com) - Specializations include General, Computers, and Honors.
  • Department of Arts (B.A.) - Programs in History, Economics, Political Science, and more.
  • Department of Computer Applications (BCA) - A comprehensive program focusing on software development and IT.

Each department is led by experienced faculty members and provides a modern curriculum to prepare students for their future careers.
